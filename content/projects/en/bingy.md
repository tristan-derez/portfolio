## Bingy

---

### Description

Bingy is a movie and TV show tracking app currently in development. It lets you create an account, manage your watchlist, rate and review movies or shows, and keep track of everything you've watched. You can also customize your profile with a bio, profile picture, and banners from your favorite movies and TV shows.

### Coming soon

- TV show progress tracking, so you never lose track of where you left off
- Reminders (when a film/tv show is available on a streaming platform, when a new episode of a tv show is out...)
- A community space to read, comment on, and follow other users’ reviews and activity
- A React Native app instead of a PWA
